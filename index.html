Gresso frames are made in our factory from a single sheet of titanium using our own single-cut technology. This enables us to produce glasses, which are the lightest and most comfortable, while still being incredibly durable.
Engineered for Headsets and Helmets
Ultra-thin titanium ear hooks with soft tips made of hypoallergenic silicone fit snugly without causing discomfort while wearing a helmet or headset. The titanium nose pads are adjustable allowing you to fine-tune the glasses for the perfect fit.
FAA Recommended Non-polarized Lenses by Zeiss
High-quality nylon lenses not only make the glasses lighter, they also ensure 100% clarity of vision. Non-polarized grey shades, recommended for use by pilots, are available in the collection.
Totally Unbreakable
Made with titanium, a material which is 2 times stronger than steel, Gresso glasses are incredibly durable, easily retain their shape, and are unbreakable. We provide a lifetime warranty on the frame.
